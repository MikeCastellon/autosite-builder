Film Technology

Not all film is
created equal.

⬡ Premium Tier

Nano-Ceramic Series

Our flagship product. No metal content means zero signal interference — your GPS, phone, and radar detector work perfectly. Superior heat rejection through nano-ceramic particle technology bonded in multi-layer construction. This is what the enthusiast community installs.

UV Rejection99%
IR Heat RejectionUp to 80%
Signal InterferenceNone
WarrantyLifetime
◈ Mid Tier

Carbon Series

Carbon-layer construction delivers a true matte-black appearance that won't fade or turn purple. Excellent heat rejection, no metal content. The most popular choice for daily drivers.

UV Rejection99%
IR RejectionUp to 60%
LookFlat / Matte
WarrantyLifetime
◇ Entry Tier

Dyed Polyester

Entry-level film that delivers privacy and basic UV protection. Heat rejection is limited compared to ceramic, but it's a solid choice for budget builds and rear-only applications.

The Install

Precision is
the standard.

// 01
📐

Consultation

We assess your vehicle, discuss goals, confirm legal VLT for your state, and recommend the right film tier for your needs and budget.

// 02
🧽

Surface Prep

Every glass surface is meticulously cleaned and decontaminated in our climate-controlled bay. Dust-free environment means zero contamination under film.

// 03
✂️

Precision Cut

We use a computerized plotter to cut film from a database of 25,000+ vehicle templates. No razor blades on your glass. Ever.

// 04
🔬

Install & Inspect

Wet installation, heat-gun forming, edge sealing, and final inspection under UV lamp. We don't hand off a car until it's perfect.
